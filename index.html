CloseConnect

Long lasting in person friendships

Try Our Prototype!

Mission

- Create and facilitate healthy relationships with people
- Focus on meeting people in person instead of online
- Continue interacting with people they have met
- Make plans to interact with other users
- Have less negative emotions towards meeting new people
- Reduce stigma towards people who are lonely or isolated

How It Works

Sign up onto the app and input your interests. The app uses your interests to find people who are physically close to you. When the app finds a match it notifies you and encourages you to interact with that person through a notification. The notification is sent to both people so that both people are encouraged to talk to each other. The app also includes suggestions about what to say based on the interests to have icebreakers.
